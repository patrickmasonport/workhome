Patrick Mason Port

Makgeolli

막걸리

Summary

Korean rice wine

Ingredients

Quantity

Sweet rice

1KG

Nuruk

100g

Filtered water

1L

Recipe

  1. Wash then soak rice for 2-4 hours.
  2. Drain and steam for 40 minutes.
  3. Dry rice for 2 hours.
  4. While drying, mix water and nuruk.
  5. Add dry rice to water/nuruk.
  6. Leave open to air and stir twice a day for 3 days.
  7. Seal and leave for 7-ish days.
  8. Filter through cheesecloth and bottle.
